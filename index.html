About EduNotes

EduNotes is an innovative application designed to help students and professionals transform their lecture recordings into structured, easy-to-review notes. Our AI-powered system automatically transcribes, translates, and organizes your spoken content into clear, categorized notes.

Our Mission

We believe that learning should be accessible to everyone, regardless of language barriers or note-taking skills. Our mission is to make education more efficient by automating the tedious parts of note-taking while preserving the important content.

How It Works

1

Record

Record your lecture or upload an audio file in multiple Indian languages.

2

Process

Our AI processes the audio into text and translates it if needed.

3

Organize

The content is automatically structured into clear, categorized notes.

4

Review

Study from well-formatted notes or listen to the audio version.
